Monster Survivors

Game Features

  • 🎮 Intense survival action gameplay
  • ⚔️ Multiple character abilities and upgrades
  • 🎯 Simple controls, challenging mechanics
  • 🏆 Compete for high scores

How to Play

  • 🎯 Use WASD or arrow keys to move
  • 🔄 Automatically attack nearby enemies
  • 📈 Collect experience orbs to level up
  • 💪 Choose power-ups to enhance your character
  • ⏰ Survive as long as possible!

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an exciting survival action game that puts your skills to the test. Face endless waves of challenging monsters while collecting power-ups and upgrading your character's abilities. With its roguelike elements and progressive difficulty, each playthrough offers a unique experience.

The game features intuitive controls and deep strategic elements, making it easy to pick up but challenging to master. Choose your upgrades wisely and create powerful combinations to survive longer and achieve higher scores.
